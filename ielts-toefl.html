Listening Practice

  • Identify the main idea from a short dialogue.
  • List two key details mentioned by the speaker.
  • Choose the best summary of the audio clip.

Reading Practice

  • Find the heading that matches paragraph 2.
  • Locate the statistic mentioned in paragraph 4.
  • Determine the author’s purpose.

Writing Practice

  • Write a 150-word summary of a chart or process.
  • Outline a response to an opinion question.

Speaking Practice

  • Describe a place you enjoy studying.
  • Explain a skill you want to improve and why.
